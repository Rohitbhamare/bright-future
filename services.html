Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a cab?

You can book through our website, call us at +91 9595 120405, or WhatsApp us. We're available 24/7 for bookings.

Are your drivers licensed and verified?

Yes, all our drivers are licensed, experienced, and background-verified. They undergo regular training for customer service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and online bank transfers. Payment can be made before or after the trip.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, we provide child seats on request. Please mention this requirement while booking to ensure availability.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify bookings up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellation charges may apply for last-minute changes.

Are tolls and parking included in the fare?

Tolls and parking charges are additional unless specified otherwise. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden charges.

Do you provide outstation services?

Yes, we provide outstation services across India. We offer both one-way and round-trip options with competitive pricing.

What safety measures do you follow?

All vehicles are regularly sanitized, drivers wear masks, and we maintain social distancing protocols. GPS tracking is available for all trips.
