Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and booking process

How can I book a cab?

You can book through our website contact form, call us at +91 9595 120405, or WhatsApp us. We're available 24/7 for bookings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and online bank transfers. Payment can be made before or after the trip as per your convenience.

Do you provide outstation services?

Yes, we provide outstation services across India. We offer both one-way and round-trip options with competitive pricing and experienced drivers.

Are your vehicles insured?

Yes, all our vehicles are fully insured and regularly maintained. We also provide comprehensive travel insurance options for long-distance trips.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify bookings up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellation charges may apply for last-minute changes.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, we provide child seats on request. Please mention this requirement while booking to ensure availability for your travel date.

What about tolls and parking charges?

Tolls and parking charges are additional unless specified otherwise in package deals. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden charges.

Do you offer corporate packages?

Yes, we offer special corporate packages for employee transportation, business meetings, and corporate events with discounted rates.
